# ADM — Purpose

ADM exists to enable persistent, meaningful narrative experiences in a way that current AI systems do not.

Many existing AI-driven interactions (e.g. character chat systems) provide moment-to-moment engagement, but lack:

- continuity over time  
- shared world state  
- lasting consequences  
- evolving relationships  

As a result, experiences feel isolated and non-persistent.

---

## What ADM Enables

ADM is designed to provide:

- **continuity**  
  interactions persist and build over time  

- **consequence**  
  actions affect the world, characters, and future outcomes  

- **world coherence**  
  experiences take place within structured, evolving environments  

- **ongoing narrative**  
  stories do not end — they continue, branch, and recombine  

---

## Core Idea

ADM bridges the gap between:
- isolated AI interaction  
and  
- persistent, immersive worlds

It allows players to:
- explore identity through their Ven  
- engage with evolving systems of meaning  
- remain inside a world that remembers, reacts, and grows  

---

ADM is not just about generating stories.

It is about:
> sustaining them over time in a way that remains interactive, coherent, and alive.
